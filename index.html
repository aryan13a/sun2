Philosophy
"We believe a home should feel like an extension of the soul. Our design language merges tactile simplicity with confident refinement — balancing cream boucle textures, raw walnut wood, and warm Mediterranean tones."

Inspired by Mediterranean architecture, natural textiles, and the quiet luxury of clean lines, boutique studio Sun Soul Style creates spaces that feel both elevated and deeply lived-in. Led by designer Keshavi Gupta and based in Jaipur, Rajasthan, we specialize in high-end residential interiors that honor natural light, organic materials, and hand-crafted details.
